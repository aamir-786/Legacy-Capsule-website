Frequently Asked Questions

How do I customize the PDF templates?

Our PDF templates are designed to be easily customizable. After purchase, you can add text, images, audio, and video content directly into the fillable fields. Use our AI dictation tool to add voice content, or upload your own media files.

Can I add my own photos, videos, and audio?

Absolutely! All our templates support multimedia content. You can upload photos, videos (MP4), audio files (MP3, WAV), and even documents. Each template has designated spaces for different types of media.

How does the AI dictation feature work?

Our AI dictation tool uses advanced speech-to-text technology. Simply click the microphone button, speak your memories or stories, and the text will be automatically transcribed and added to your PDF template.

What file formats do you support?

We support common image formats (JPG, PNG, GIF), audio formats (MP3, WAV, AAC), video formats (MP4, MOV), and documents (PDF, DOC, DOCX). Each media file should be under 50MB for optimal performance.

How does the reseller program work?

Our reseller program allows you to sell our templates to your audience and earn commission. We provide marketing materials, tracking links, and dedicated support. Choose from Basic (30%), Pro (40%), or Partner (50%) commission levels.

Is payment secure?

Yes! We use Stripe for secure payment processing. Your payment information is encrypted and never stored on our servers. We accept all major credit cards and PayPal.
